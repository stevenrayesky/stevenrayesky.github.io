Hello World! Do I even blog? Well, it's been awhile since my last post, but I wanted to mention that I have started a podcast, [Philly Dev Podcast!](http://phillydevpodcast.com) It's my way of adding to the great community of developers that exists in Philadelphia! What is it? Every other week or so I interview a Philly dev and let them tell their story:
Who they are, what they do, where they have been, where they want to go and what they love about the city we inhabit!

Pretty great, right? I am always looking for folks to interview, so if you have a dev in mind (or you think you would be a good guest!) send me an email at phillydevpodcast@gmail.com.

Update about me: I graduated from NYCDA in March and I am looking for full time employment while continuing to code and build some sites! [Great time to be alive](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9UaJAnnipkY) as they say.
